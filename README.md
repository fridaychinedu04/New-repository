### **Introduction**  
In this project, i will be analysing records of car accidents in New York City from January to August, 2020...  

### **Objectives**  
1. To compare the percentage of total accident by `month`  and observe if there is any noticeable seasonal pattern  
1. To break down the frequency of the accident down into:  
    - `days` of the week  
    - `hours` of the day  
1. To determine when accidents occur most frequently based on:  
    - `days` of the week  
    - `hours` of the day  
1. To check if there are more accidents during weekdays than weekends
